DeBoleia: A Success Story Born from a Need

The idea of DeBoleia was born in January 2025, as a response to a concrete need: its founders, Frederico Bajouco, Ebenezer Marquezine, Leila Dantas, Daniel Sampaio, and Ana Vilarinho, needed to return home, but there were no train tickets left. Eventually, they managed to have a friend pick them up by car.

During the journey, they realized that most of the vehicles they saw on the road were almost empty. This sparked the idea of creating a social network that would connect drivers with empty seats to passengers who needed to travel the same route.
